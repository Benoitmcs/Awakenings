Can Good Psychiatry Really Help You Take Less Medication?

Psychiatry is often associated with overmedication and polypharmacy. This article explores how/when medication can be reduced, and how lifestyle changes can sometimes reduce of the need for certain medications.

The journey to wellness begins not with perfection, but with small, intentional actions. Psychiatric medications are powerful, but their effectiveness is significantly influenced by daily habits—particularly movement and nutrition. Even minimal efforts, like a 10-minute walk or choosing whole foods, can meaningfully support mental health and medication outcomes.
